KrezzCut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

KrezzCut respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how KrezzCut collects, uses, and protects information when you use the KrezzCut mobile app and related services.

Information We May Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Account information
  • Order details
  • Customization details needed to create your product

We may also collect limited technical information needed to operate and improve the app, such as device or diagnostic information.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders and payments
  • Generate and fulfill custom products
  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate about orders, shipping, or account activity
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Face Scan and Product Generation

KrezzCut uses face scanning and related measurements to help generate custom grooming products. Information used for product creation is only used to provide the requested service and improve the user experience.

Payments

Payments may be processed by third-party payment providers. KrezzCut does not store full payment card details on its own servers.

Sharing of Information

We may share information only as needed with service providers that help us operate the app, process payments, manufacture products, fulfill orders, or provide customer support. We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide services, fulfill orders, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request updates or deletion of your account information, subject to legal or operational requirements.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

KrezzCut is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
